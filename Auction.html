Rules for the cricket auction

  • Each user should pick a maximum of 25 players in both International and National section.
  • The team must have 7 foreign players only. The cricketers from the Indian subcontinent will be auctioned in the Asia quota. Each team must have 2-3 players of the Asian quota.The players of Asian quota will not be considered as foreign players.
  • The user should utilize the given amount to pick the 25 best players for his team. No extra coins will be provided.
  • There will be a lot of sub-categories in those rounds. The major divisions for the rounds will be - Capped Players, Uncapped Players and Unsold Players.
